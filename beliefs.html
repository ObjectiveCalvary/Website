Our Beliefs

Bodily Resurrection

At the core of our faith, we affirm the historical truth of Jesus Christ's resurrection from the dead, which demonstrates His victory over sin and death.

Authority of Scripture

We hold the Bible as the authoritative and inspired Word of God, providing guidance and wisdom for our lives and faith.

Salvation Through Grace

We believe that salvation is a gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone, and not by our own efforts.

The Trinity

We affirm the doctrine of the Trinity—God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit—as one divine being in three persons.

Loving the Sinner, Hating the Sin

We embrace the teaching of loving every individual as Christ loves us, recognizing the inherent worth and dignity of every person. While we stand firm against sinful behaviors, we also strive to show compassion and grace, seeking restoration and transformation through the power of Christ's love.

Second Coming and Final Judgment

We anticipate the second coming of Jesus Christ, when He will fully establish His kingdom, and we affirm the future reality of a final judgment where all will give an account.
