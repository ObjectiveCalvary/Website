    What we do?

    Take a look at what we have to offer.

    Sermons

    Engaging Bible-based sermons tailored for teenagers and young adults, addressing their unique season of life and empowering them with God's word.

    Small Groups

    Facilitating discipleship and fostering meaningful relationships, our program organizes small groups based on age and gender, guided by dedicated mentors.

    Weekly Devotionals

    Access a wealth of Online Bible materials, quizzes, PDFs, and videos, all designed to nurture weekly growth through the use of the OC strand curriculum.

    Games

    Fun-filled and all-inclusive games that excite everyone, seamlessly weaving together teamwork, individual talents, and a strong sense of fellowship.

    Social

    Find a welcoming community of like-minded Christians, centered around their devotion to Jesus Christ, and delighting in joyful fellowship within the Lord's house together.

    Interactive Learning

    Engage with the Bible through the utilization of 3D-printed replicas and interactive modeling software, illuminating the reality of scripture.
